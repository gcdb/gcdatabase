KOF Britannia

■ Event Duration
24 September 2020 (Thursday) after maintenance ~ 8 October 2020 (Thursday) before maintenance

■ Event Details
1. Obtain [Fighters Coin] from Boss Battle, Death Match, Tower of Challenge, Training Grotto and Final Boss Omega Rugal (max of 30/day); exchange 1 silver coin for 5 [Fighters Coin] in the coin shop (5 times/day).
2. Use the coins to start the event.
3. You will choose between the different tournament difficulty and participate in it. There will be teams restrictions (categories of heroes to use)
4. There are rewards for repeated clears and first time clear. (First time clear rewards include: Diamonds, SR Evolution Pendant, SSR/UR Treasure Chest, SSR Evolution Pendants, Anvils, Hammer of Vaizel, Equipment Engraving Stone, Super Awakening Coin; repeated clear rewards are [Proof of God of Fighting] which can be used in the Exchange Shop).
5. During the tournament, there will also be [NEW CHALLENGER] intruding into the battle field, they will consist of commonly seen heroes used in PVP. During the random intrusion, stats of base R units will be boosted by 30% and SR units by 20%. Defeating the challengers will drop Awakening Stones and [Proof of God of Fighting]/
