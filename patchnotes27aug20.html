20M DL Roulette Event

■ Event Duration
27 August 2020 (Thursday) after maintenance ~ 10 September 2020 (Thursday) before maintenance

■ Event Details
Obtain [20M DL Commemorative Coin] by clearing Main Stage, Free Stage, Boss Battle, Death Match, SP Dungeon, Training Grotto. Use the coins to play the roulette. You can reset the roulette up to 7 times.

You can also use 1 silver coin to exchange for 1 [20M DL Commemorative Coin] 5 times a day, use 1 gold coin to exchange for 5 [20M DL Commemorative Coin] for 1 time a day.

Rewards from the roulette and resetting the roulette include SSR [Leftover Knight] Captain Hawk, SSR outfit, cosmetic and weapon for big Diane, Super Awakening coins, and various character growth rewards.

■ System Changes ■

1. Removal of Ult Level Limitation for Super Awakening (SA)

Ult level restriction is now removed when Super Awakening a hero. You are now able to super awaken by consuming the required amount of coins depending on the ult level.
For example, the amount of coins required to awaken a hero with lvl 1 ult are as follows:
SA phase 1: [SA Coin] x3
SA phase 2: [SA Coin] x6 (existing 3 + additional 3)
SA phase 3: [SA Coin] x9 (existing 3 + additional 3)

2. Added Recommended Friend refresh function in friends list.

3. Event List

4. [Death Match] Tutorial Clear Rewards

Upon clearing quests that lead to unlocking a new Death Match, the rewards have been changed.
Previously clearing Event Quest Invader of the Fairy King's Forest will grant you 1 diamond, you will now get SSR [Overpower] Slater.
Previously clearing Chapter 6 Episode 73-1 will grant you Part 1 GSSR ticket x1, you will now get SSR [Forest Guardian] King the Fairy King.
Previously clearing Chapter 7 Episode 121 wil grant you Part 1 SSR ticket x1, you will now get SSR [Nunchuck] Adventure Ban.

5. [Achievement] mission progress changes

Some of the missions have progress bars that are unachievable, the conditions for those missions are now changed.

6. [Knighthood Shop] System Changes

1. Purchaseable limit reset timing changes
