Boss Skills Info

● For convenience's sake, we will name the forms: base form, red form and purple form.

● Puringpooh changes his attack pattern depending on the damage he received from you during the first 3 turns. If he receives more than 300,000 damage in the first 3 turns, he will switch to red form, if not, he will switch to purple form.

● After 3 turns, he switches back to the base form and the cycle repeats.

● All 3 forms take 50% less damage from counters, removes 1 debuff from himself at the start of his turn, and are immune to HP reduction, HP-related stat reduction and corrosion effects.

● In Poringpooh's base form, he has a single target reduce ult gauge damaging skill, AOE skill that increases his ult gauge and an AOE skill that seals healing skill.

● Ideally, you will not want Puringpooh to go into purple form. In the purple form, he has a single target poison skill, a single target removing buff damage skill, a single target damaging stun skill and a cleanse and DEF buff skill.

● In Puringpooh's red form, he has an AOE detonation skill, AOE removing and sealing stances, AOE DEF-related stat reduction damage skill, AOE ult gauge seal. As these are all AOE skills, you will not take a lot of damage from this form.

● Base form and purple form both have weakness on their ults, while red form's ult is an AOE rank down with ult gauge reduction (same as Lillia and Howzer ult).

● When Puringpooh is the weak form, he will switch back to base form after 2 turns. When Puringpooh is in the strong form, he will switch back to base form after 3 turns. And then the cycle repeats.
