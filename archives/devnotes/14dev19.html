14th KOO P Dev Notes (27 Dec)

1. Grand Cross New Year Event

We want everyone to go into 2020 with a bang and so we have prepared a lot of gifts and new year events.

[Event] New Year Login Bonus

To commemorate the beginning of a new year, we will be giving out gems to all players for 3 days. Please visit the Boar Hat during the event.

[Event] New Year Fortune Telling (Omikuji) Event

We are going to have a fortune telling event for the players, and depending on the results, players will receive corresponding gifts.

[Event] [New Year Training Cave] Event

After completing the regular [Training Cave], there will be a special new year cave that players will be able to earn gems, and various items such as awakening stone upon clearing.

[Event] Event Hawk Pass

With the new year, another improved Hawk Pass will be available.

The new Hawk Pass will be opened for 2 weeks, with NORMAL having 25 levels and SPECIAL having 35 levels.

Other well received events such as [Gear Breakdown Success Rate Up], [Character Level Up Rate Up], [Viezel Hammer Failure Rate 0%] will also see a comeback.

2. Movie - Prisoners of the Sky

The story for the movie will be added!

3. 2020 Grand Cross

A lot more content will be added in the year of 2020.

First, we will be adjusting the current content.

As mentioned in previous dev notes, we will be making improvements to Geared PvP.

In addition, existing contents such as [Guild], [Raids], [Super Boss] will also receive improvements to make the gameplay more interesting.

For example, we will implement achievement system to the contents, and make communication with other players a more enjoyable experience.

And of course, we are also preparing the story of season 3 and Grand Cross's original story for all players to immerse in.

To kick it off, we will be introducing the movie, Prisoners of the Sky. The story of Hawk and the sins will continue in 2020.

Furthermore, on top of [Super Boss Battles], we also plan to gradually add more game modes to reward and challenge veteran players.

How would it look like in Twigo's perspective during his fight with Meliodas?

How difficult would it be if we can experience the "real" flower garden that we once battled in against Hendrickson?

[Challenge! Main HELL Stage (temp name)] is in the making. In the future, 4 players will have to work together to defeat a giant demon.

We are looking at how to play the game in multiple ways and trying to approach the game meaningfully.

We are also actively hearing our players' voices for quality of life updates. And with that, we are going to add a team tab for HELL raids.

As for powering up the gears, we will also make QoL updates so that it will be more convenient for the players.

However, with new challenges and improvements there is the possibility of introducing new bugs, we are trying our best to take care and minimize the chances of that happening.

We have already planned out the updates in 2020 even till the end of the year, please do look forward to it.

We will strive our best in 2020, and bring even more fun to our players.

4. Closing Off 2019

Since the game's release in summer 2019, many players were able to enjoy the game.

Although I was excited to share new experience with the players, I have also noted that there are a lot more to work on.

But even among all of this, I would love to thank everyone who cherished and nurtured Grand Cross with the encouragement and criticism.

We would not be having the contents and the community without everyone's support.

We will continue to value our players' opinions and deliver even more enjoyable contents to you.

Thank you so much once again for spending 2019 with Grand Cross, and please continue to love Grand Cross in 2020.
