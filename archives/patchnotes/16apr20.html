5. Overall CC Buff Effect Added
Added the screen to check for the current in use buff effect

※ The buff only applies to battle contents
※ The buff does not change the account CC
※ The buff will affect team CC
※ The buff does not stack
If your CC is between 1.5mil to 2mil, you will get 4.5% HP buff

6. Gear gacha auto summon and salvage system

A function that auto summons on gear gacha is added.

You can now set the gear type and rarity to auto salvage the gear after summoning.

The max number of auto summoning can be set according to the amount of gold you have.

7. Achievements and Title System Changes
Added the screen to check for the current in use buff effect
1. Adding the achievement menu in [Challenge Mission]
2. Added an achievement for Vaizel PVP
3. Changed condition for certain achievements
Before: you can only get the title if you have exactly the same amount of items and gold
After: You can obtain the title by possessing more than the stated condition

8. Changes to recommended friend
You will not get recommended friend on the friend list menu if you have maxed out the friend list

9. Jukebox Composition System
When composing with jukebox, you will not see unnecessary backgrounds

10. Veronica Buff System Changes
After clearing Chapter 7 of the main story, Veronica's buff (20% more gold when selling items) will be applied permanently. Before clearing chapter 7, you can still invite her. You will not be able to invite her to the tavern after you have cleared chapter 7.

11. 7 Disasters Lillia Raid Extended
Raid is extended till 29 Apr (before maintenance)
Exchange shop is extended till 3 May 23:59
You are now able to have a preset team for each attribute of the raid
We are currently planning for 7 Disaster Season 2 due to popular demands
