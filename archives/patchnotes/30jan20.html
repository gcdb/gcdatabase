■ <10 Commandments> Estarossa Skills Info (rank 3)

Skill 1 - Reverse Blade: Inflict 360% of this unit's ATK to a single enemy. The target will not be able to use healing skills for 2 turns.

Commandment of Benevolence : When allies and enemies are under attack, decrease the ATK-related stats of the attacker by 20%. (activates only when Estarossa joins the battle, this effect does not activate in Raids)

■ [Demon Clan Elite] Estarossa Skills Info (rank 3)

Skill 1 - Guillotine: Rush a single enemy with 500% of this unit's ATK.

Skill 2 - Full Counter: Take a stance for 2 turns, taunt all enemies and counter attacks with 600% of this unit's ATK.

Ult - Blackout: For one turn, take a stance. Perform a preemptive attack before allies receive damage, deal 300% of this unit's ATK to the enemies, and gain immunity to skill card(s).

Commandment of Benevolence : When allies and enemies are under attack, decrease the ATK-related stats of the attacker by 20%. (activates only when Estarossa joins the battle, this effect does not activate in Raids)

Passive/Unique - Valiant Heart: When an ally uses stance, cleanse all debuffs on the ally and grant the target immunity to debuffs for 2 turns (excluding for ults).

■ 3. Costume Upgrade System
You will be able to use the costume upgrade system to enhance the ATK, DEF and HP of the costume.
The costume upgrade material will be different for each costume part. The stats will also increase with more consumption of the upgrade materials.
If you raise the stats of the costume to the max value, the rarity of the costume will be increased by 1 rank.
All costumes can be upgraded to UR, and UR costumes can only have increase in stats.
The amount of costume upgrades will be dependant on the rarity of the costume. The stats that increase with each upgrade are the same regardless of the rarity.
※ R rarity costume can be upgraded to UR but will require more upgrade materials.

■ Costume Upgrade Material ■
※ Obtainable via HELL raids and [Costume Upgrade Pack]
※ There will be more ways of obtaining the materials in the future.
