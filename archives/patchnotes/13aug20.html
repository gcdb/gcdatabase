Update Details

1. Liones Fireworks Festival Event

Event [Liones Fireworks Festival]

■ Event Duration
13 August 2020 (Thursday) after maintenance ~ 27 August 2020 (Thursday) before maintenance

■ How to Participate
Tap the FIREWORKS icon at the top left corner when you are in the Boar Hat.

■ Note
・ Making the fireworks (top rectangle): Clearing certain stages or completing [Event Missions] will grant you materials to make the fireworks.
・ Event Missions (middle rectangle): Event missions changes daily and clearing the missions will grant you materials to make firework.
・ Exchange shop (bottom rectangle): You can exchange secondary fireworks mats for various items.
