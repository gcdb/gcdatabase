9. New PVP Mode [Grand Brawl]

■ Grand Brawl 3 Team vs 3 Team Rules
1. You will form a total of 3 teams
2. All of the 3 teams will be used to fight against other players
3. The brawl is a best of three, first players to win 2 games win it all

■ Note
All battles are done in AUTO mode, you can immediately check the result of the matches by pressing the SKIP button.
・ If you win against someone with a higher rank, you will take over the opponent's rank.
※ If you win against someone with a lower rank, you will only gain victory reward gauge.
・ When the victory reward gauge is filled to the MAX, you will get [Grand Brawl Box] as reward.
※ Rewards are based on the current ranking.
For example, if you dropped to 2nd place after reaching 1st place, you'll receive the reward for 2nd place.
・ If you obtained a higher rank after receiving the reward, you will be not be able to accept the reward till after 22h has passed.
For example, if you reached 1st place after getting the 2nd place reward, you cannot obtain the reward for 1st place yet.
・ The list of opponents are updated hourly, or after each battle.
※ You will need to have more than 1 hero on every registered team.
※ You can only register your teams 3 times/day.
※ Gear and stats information on the registered team will not change after registration.
For example, if you registered a SSR lvl 50 hero and then evolving the hero to UR lvl 60, the registered hero will remain at SSR lvl 50 until you re-register the team.
※ Disconnecting in the middle of the match will result in a loss.
※ Future updates will bring in cooking.

Grand Brawl Shop
By participating in the Grand Brawl, you will earn Brawl Coins. Brawl coins can be used in the shop to exchange for various items. The upper tier shop will open if you purchase more items. note that purchasing 5 of the same item in one go will treat it as 1 purchase count.
