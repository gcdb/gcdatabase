Link to Escanor Fate Ult Animation

■ Escanor Skills Info (rank 3)

Skill 1 - Blazing Sun: Inflict amplifying damage of 450% of this unit's ATK to a single enemy.

Skill 2 - Charge & Fire: Inflict 250% of this unit's ATK to all enemies. Reduce ultimate gauges of the targets by 3.

Ult - Punishment: Inflict 840% of this unit's ATK to a single enemy.

Fate Ult - Gentleman's Dignity: Inflict 960% of this unit's ATK to a single enemy.

Passive/Unique - Fire: When this unit joins the battle, increase the HP-related stats of this unit by 50% for 3 turns.

■ Meliodas Skills Info (rank 3)

Skill 1 - Chain Blow: Inflict 360% of this unit's ATK to a single enemy. The target will not be able to gain any ultimate gauge for 1 turn.

Skill 2 - Devil's Fire: Inflict 180% of this unit's ATK to all enemies. Infect the targets for 2 turn.

Ult - Hellbuster: Inflict 385% of this unit's ATK to the weakness of a single enemy.

Fate Ult - Devil Assult: Inflict 440% of this unit's ATK to the weakness of a single enemy.

Passive/Unique - Declaration of Death: During your turn, if an enemy character dies with Meliodas dealing the final blow, increase Meliodas's ultimate gauge by the no. of ultimate gauge the enemy had.

■ Elizabeth Skills Info (rank 3)

Skill 1 - Royal Dignity: Inflict 250% of this unit's ATK to all enemies. Restores HP to the ally with the lowest HP by 60% of the damage dealt.

Skill 2 - Blessing Light: Cleanse all debuffs on all allies, grant immunity to debuffs for all allies and increase their DEF-related stats by 30% for 3 turns.

Ult - Life Authority: Heal all allies's HP for 420% based on this unit's ATK. Increase all allies' ultimate gauge by 2.

Fate Ult - Goddess Garden: Heal all allies's HP for 480% based on this unit's ATK. Increase all allies' ultimate gauge by 2.

Passive/Unique - Descending Light: Every time an ally gets healed by Elizabeth, incrase the recovery rate of all allies by 10% (stacks up to 10 times).

■ Jericho Skills Info (rank 3)

Skill 1 - Swift Dash: Inflict 300% of this unit's ATK to a single enemy, explode after 1 turn.

Skill 2 - Tornado Slash: Inflict 300% of this unit's ATK to a single enemy. Decrease the target's HP-related stats by 30% for 2 turns.

Ult - Silent Split: Cut a single enemy with 560% of this unit's ATK.

Fate Ult - Chain Slash: Cut a single enemy with 640% of this unit's ATK.

Passive/Unique - Sharp Sense: Whenever any allies crit the enemy when using skills, increase the crit chance of all allies by 5% (stacks up to 10 times).
