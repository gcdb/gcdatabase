Titan Expulsion Battle

■ Event Duration
29 June 2020 (Monday) after maintenance ~ 16 July 2020 (Thursday) before maintenance

■ Event Details
Choose 8 characters and retake back the bases that Titans have occupied. Clearing the event stages will open up event free stages. Clearing the free stages will earn you [Titan Report]. The [Titan Report] will be used to enter [Titan Extermination Battle]. [Titan Report] can also be obtained from [SP Dungeon], [Boss Battle] and [Death Match]

Titan Extermination Battle

■ Event Duration
29 June 2020 (Monday) after maintenance ~ 16 July 2020 (Thursday) before maintenance

■ Event Details
[Titan Report] of the party leader will be consumed to enter the battle. Play the battle real time with 2~4 players to defeat the Colossal Titan. Upon defeating the Colossal Titan, [3D Maneuver Gear Shards] will drop and can be used to exchange for various items.
