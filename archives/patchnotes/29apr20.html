7. Knighthood Battle (Beta)

■ Beta Duration
10 May 2020 (Sunday) 00:31 ~ 2 June 2020 (Tuesday) 23:59

■ Selection Period
10 May 2020 (Sunday) 00:31 ~ 10 May 2020 (Sunday) 23:59

Based on the Knighthood points obtained, top 16 Knighthoods will be selected to enter Champion League and Master League.

■ Matching Period
11 May 2020 (Monday) 00:31 ~ 11 May 2020 (Monday) 23:59

Matching for Champion League and Master League will take place.

■ Battle Period
12 May 2020 (Tuesday) 00:31 ~ 1 June 2020 (Monday) 23:59

Knighthoods will have 1 day to prepare for the battle and each round of battle will take place over 2 days. You will battle Knighthood in the same league once. It will take 21 days to finish one rotation of 7 battles.

■ Final Tally Period
2 June 2020 (Tuesday) 00:00 ~ 2 June 2020 (Tuesday) 23:59

Based on the result of the battle final ranking will be tallied.

■ Participation Eligibility
Top 16 Knighthoods in the Knighthood Boss ranking will be selected to participate in the Beta testing.

■ Knighthood Battle (Beta) Rules
1. Once the top 16 Knighthoods have been selected, the Beta campaign will commence.
2. Rank 1~8 will participate in the Champion League and Rank 9~16 will participate in the Master League.
3. Knighthood will fight against other Knighthoods once each in their own league, altogether 7 battles will take place.
4. When the battle is over, the Knighthood with the highest capture point will be the winner for that round, and that will contribute to the final score (Win: 3 points, Draw: 1 point, lose: 0 point)
5. After 7 rounds of battle, based on the final point the Knighthood can be promoted, stay put or relegated.
・ Champion League 1~4: Remains in Champion League
・ Champion League 5~8: Relegated to Master League
・ Master League 1~4: Promoted to Champion League
・ Master League 5~8: Drop out
6. At the end fo the beta, rewards will be given based on final ranking
7. To replace the dropped out Knighthoods, the 4Knighthoods that ranked top 4 of the previous week's Knighthood boss will participate in the next round of Beta (excluding the 12 Knighthoods who are already qualified)

■ Knighthood Battle (Beta) Special Items
Special items for Knighthood Battle can be purchased from Knighthood shop to aid in the battle.
1. Attack Squad Expansion Item (expands up to 8)
2. Defense Squad Expansion Item (expands up to 2)
※ Items can only be used for 1 round
3. Defense-specific buff items
When forming a defense team, you can use buff items to raise the stats of the team. The item can be obtained via Attribute [Essence Box]. There are 7 essences in the box that will raise the stats based on the character's attribute. You can use the essence in 3 slots and the same essence can't be used again.

■ Knighthood Battle (Beta) Point System
1. Occupation Points
Occupying each base will earn you 100 points each, you can occupy all 30 bases.
Capturing an entire region will earn bonus occupation points, and the points is preset by the opponent's Knighthood leader.
The Knighthood with higher cumulative occupation points will win the round and earn final score
※ You will not be able to see the bonus occupation point set by the opponent until you captured every base in the region.2. Season Ranking
After 7 rounds, the final score will determine the ranking.
- Win: 3 points, Draw: 1 point, lose: 0 point

■ Knighthood Battle (Beta) Personal Reward
For each base you captured you will earn 20 Knighthood Coins
