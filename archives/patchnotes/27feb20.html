2. Reverse Stage

■ How to Participate
Tap on Reverse Stage in the battle menu

Reverse Stage will be unlocked upon clearing Chapter 12-207.

・ Ally characters and enemies will be swapped from Chapter 1 - 6.5
・ The enemies you have once fought will be needed as the required character to enter the stage
・ There are altogether 25 stages, and you will get reward for clearing the stage for the first time
・ There are different stage missions available, and upon clearing the missions you will gain stars. You will earn corresponding rewards based on the stars obtained.
・ You do not have to do the battles in chronological order
・ AP willbe consumed

※ The battles will be ungeared
※ You will be able to put on link characters
※ You will not be able to use friend rep

3. Addition of 4 New Characters

Link to Derieri Ult

■ <Ten Commandments> Purity Derieri Skills Info (rank 3)

Skill 1 - Darkness Hunt: Inflict 600% of this unit's ATK to a single enemy.

Skill 2 - Injection: Increase the base stats of this unit by 50% for 3 turns, gain dodge for 1 turn.

Ult - Combo Star: Inflict 630% amplifying damage to a single enemy.

Commandment - Purity: Decrease the damage of ranged attacks of all allies and enemies receive in PvP by 35% (this effect only activates when Derieri joins the battle, this effect does not affect ultimate skills).

Passive/Unique - Battle Power: When this unit uses a skill, increase her ATK by 10%. This effect goes away when she does not use a skill (stacks up to 5 times).

Link to Derieri Ult

■ [Elite Demon] Purity Derieri Skills Info (rank 3)

Skill 1 - Chain Strike: Inflict 450% of this unit's ATK to a single enemy, reduce the target's ultimate gauge by 3.

Skill 2 - Leap Shock: Inflict 300% of this unit's ATK to all enemies, gain 2 ultimate gauges for herself.

Ult - Combo Star: Inflict 630% amplifying damage to a single enemy.

Commandment - Purity: Decrease the damage of ranged attack all allies and enemies receive in PvP by 35%.

Passive/Unique - Frontier: When enemies move their skills around, ultimate gauges will not be increased.

Link to Galland Ult

■ [Elite Demon] Truth Galland Skills Info (rank 3)

Skill 1 - Flame World: Crush a single enemy with 450% of this unit ATK.

Skill 2 - Decapitate: Remove stances and buffs from a single enemy, inflict 400% of this unit's ATK to a single enemy.

Ult - Critical Over: Increase the base stats of this unit by 50% for 3 turns. Inflict 420% of this unit's ATK to a single enemy.

Commandment - Truth: Allies and enemies that used rank up or rank down skills will be petrified for 2 turns (this effect only activates when Galland is active in the battle, the commandment does not work in raid).

Passive/Unique - Armor Break: When Strength allies use a skill, ignore 30% of the enemy's DEF (excluding Counter skill).

■ [Elite Demon] Faith Melascula Skills Info (rank 3)

Skill 1 -Pierce of Death: Scorch (for each ignite effect on the enemy, increase damage by 25%) a single enemy with 400% of this unit's ATK.

Skill 2 - Spirit Consume: For 2 turns, intake (decrease the enemy's max HP by a certain amount, and recover this unit's HP by the amount decreased) 20% of all enemies' max HP.

Ult - Soul Invitation: Inflict 560% of this unit's ATK to a single enemy, lifesteal for 40% of the damage dealt.

Commandment - Faith: Allies and enemies who did not use any skills will get 2 ignites for 5 turns at the end of every turn (this effect only activates when Melascula is active in the battle) in PvP.

Passive/Unique -Resurrection: When an ally dies, grant a buff that makes the ally stay at 1HP for 3 turns (this effect only activates once, the effect when Melascula dies).
※ The target ally will be revived with 1 HP, and the HP will not change even when attacked or healed.
※The buff can be removed
※ This effect does not activate on Melascula herself
※ If 2 allies die at the same time, the left most ally on the screen will be the target of the passive

Obtainable Location
※ The 2 Derieri will be available in the pick up banner
※ Galland and Melascula will be available for purchase in coin shop or in the special pack
※ After the pick up gacha banner ends, the respective Derieri will be added into either part 2 general pool or coin shop. Details will be announced in the future.
