Tips and strategies

Phase 1

● There are really not a lot of mechanics to Diane boss compared to the previous Final Bosses. The optimal way to clear Diane is to use an AOE Pierce comp, the preferred team is blue Lillia, blue Demon Meliodas and Zaratras (for higher points as seen on the leaderboard). If you do not have the aforementioned units, green Fat King is a good option to look into. If you want to run the F2P friendly team given above, the Elihawk has Pierce Rate subs on her ring.

● Use AOE cards to repeatedly kill the golems to soften Diane's HP and Defense.

● Although healing cards are sealed in phase 1, your team will heal back 30% of their diminished health, and Diane+golems will have their max HP reduced by 20% if the golems at the side are killed.

● You will be able to heal after you defeat Diane in phase 1.

● Ideally, you should try to save a Lillia AOE ult gauge decrease going into phase 2 to avoid getting ulted by her in turn 1. Skills used after Diane dies in phase 1 are counted towards the ult gauge, you can build up some ult gauge going into phase 2 this way.

Phase 2

● The strategy for phase 2 is the same as phase 1, just that killing the golems will reduce Diane's Defense and Resistance by 20%.

● Try not to stall for too long in phase 2 as Diane hits harder as turns go by.
