Key Units Analysis

Unit Remark

Gowther		 Gowther is to be used for ranking up other unit's skills and the AOE ult to reduce the ultimate gauge of all enemies.
King provides extra healing on his 2nd skill and cure, and together with the passive healing from Elizabeth on the sub slot, you will be able gain a fair amount of HP after every turn. His passive which cleanses himself at the start of every turn also comes in helpful. In phase 1, he will be immune to the poison effect from the illusions (the 2 magician women at the side of Merlin Boss). In phase 2, King will be immune to the freeze effect from Merlin Boss.

Arthur		 Arthur is a great buffer and immunity provider. The immunity to debuffs will allow your units to be immune to poison effect and take less damage from the weakness damage subsequently in phase 1. In phase 2, Merlin's freeze will also not work if there is an immunity buff in place. On top of the immunity, he also provides extra ATK, DEF and HP to the allies, allowing more survivability and damage output to be done to Merlin.

Escanor		 Escanor is a top tier DPS for Merlin Boss. His 2nd skill (Cruel Sun) will remove the buffs from a single target, specifically her shield. When he has max ultimate gauges, he will be immune to debuffs from the illusions in phase 1, and freeze from Merlin in phase 2.

Escanor		 Coin Escanor is busted for Merlin, pair him with Arthur to nuke a good chunk of HP off Merlin.

Helbram		 Helbram takes away Merlin's ultimate gauge(s) on his 1st skill. His ult also removes buffs from all targets. HE HITS HARD ON RANK 3 BTW (Escanor is harder tho).

Elizabeth		 Elizabeth's only duty is for her to be on the sub slot and perform passive healing from her passive. 10% healing each turn is great for survivability.

Howzer		 Howzer has a full AOE kit, therefore, he will be able to damage the illusions as well as Merlin at the same time. One of Merlin's passive in phase 1 is that for each illusion defeated, she will have -40% defense. With Howzer killing the illusions at the side, it will be considerably easier to damage Merlin. The same goes for phase 2, but instead of -40% defense, Merlin will have -40% HP for each illusion defeated.

Tips

Do aim for Merlin herself in phase 1 if you are running single target DPS. In phase 2, focus on killing the illusions at the side before targeting Merlin herself.

Do not bring in units with weakness attacks such as Red Nunchuck Ban, because Merlin and the illusions are immune to debuffs.

Do not bring in a blue unit as Merlin deals 300% more damage to blue units.

Do not bring in a demon clan unit as Merlin deals 300% more damage to demon units

Do not use units with counter, as Merlin takes 80% less damage from counters

Eat regeneration food
