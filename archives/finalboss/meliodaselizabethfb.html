Main Team Key Units Analysis

Unit Remark

Gowther		 Gowther is mainly to be used for ranking Escanor/Merlin/Jericho's skills. If you are able to get an early ultimate ready, you will be able to delay Elizabeth's ultimate skill's heal in Phase 1. Gowther is also crucial to dealing high damage when you have his passive built up, you will be able to build up the passive in Phase 1 after killing Meliodas, you can do this by attack sealing Elizabeth. Ideally, you should go in Phase 2 with at least 3 stacks of the passive for at least 30% bonus attack. If possible, you can try to run Gowther on 3x DEF with Resistance stats so Gowther will take no damage when Meliodas does his AOE in Phase 1.
Escanor will be able to get his ultimate fast when paired with Gowther. Since there are a lot of debuffing going on in both phase 1 and phase 2, the immunity to debuffs when he has MAX ult gauge helps to alleviate the burden a little. Aside from that, his ultimate skill also hits super hard combined with a rank 3 Merlin freeze.
Red Escanor is a good alternative to the green counterpart. His skills are all pure attacking cards, therefore making it easier to do damage to Meliodas in Phase 1. His Skill 2 on rank 2 and 3 removes the ult gauge of Meliodas and Elizabeth, making it great for utility. If you have Gowther rank 3 buffs, it synergises well with his Skill 1, which amplifies the damage based on the number of buffs he has. Since this super boss does not take attribute disadvantage into consideration, the ult will hit equally hard as green Escanor. It is also relatively easier to max out his ultimate skill because he is a coin shop unit.
Merlin's role in this team is to provide gold freezes so the nukers will be able to dish out damage. Her attack card sealing on the ultimate skill can also stop Elizabeth from attacking. If you have her ultimate leveled up to above 4/6, a decently geared Merlin can also act as a nuker. For players who do not have Merlin, a budget alternative is Gustaf, who has the same skill sets as Merlin aside from the ultimate skill and passive.
Jericho is also a top tier DPS when it comes to the Super Boss, however, she has slightly higher gear requirements compared to Escanor, and would ideally work best when she has a 6/6 ultimate and have 3x CDMG gear or ATK/CDMG. She has innate lifesteal so when combined with lifesteal food, she is self sustainable.

Sub Units Analysis

Unit Remark
Alioni's passive boosts the ATK-related stats of Vitality attribute units such as Escanor and Jericho by 10%. In the ideal situation, Alioni should not be present on the field to "fight". However, if Alioni do come up as a sub when one of your main team unit dies, he is still able to dish out reasonable damage and his ultimate skill can remove Meliodas' stance. A minor downside of using Alioni on the sub slot is that he has relatively low Combat Class (CP), without investing heavily into Alioni himself or having a spare set of decent gear, you might be having trouble to hit over 160k Combat Class (CP) requirements to start first in HELL.
As the alternative to Alioni and Jericho, Howzer has a lot more base Combat Class (CP) than Alioni. He provides 8% ATK, DEF and HP boost to human characters. He provides less in terms of ATK boost to the main DPS (Escanor, Jericho), but he does give them a little more survivability in terms of HP and DEF. If you have trouble reaching 160k Combat Class (CP) with Alioni, Howzer is the next best option.
Jericho is the preferred sub unit when you are running mono red team, just like Alioni.

Strategies and Tips (Green Escanor comp)

(click for sample run)

● Aim for Meliodas in Phase 1 first before moving onto Elizabeth.

● Rank up Escanor to get his ultimate off Meliodas before working on getting gold freezes.

● Both Meliodas and Elizabeth in both Phase 1 and Phase 2 are immune to Corrosion, Petrification and Stuns.
However, Meliodas in both Phases cleanse freezes at the start of his turn. On the other hand, Elizabeth does not cleanse the freeze - she is susceptible to freezes.

● Phase 1's Meliodas takes 50% more damage in the counter stance, and he WILL NOT counterattack if you are using attacking cards (non-debuff cards). If you are running Escanor, you can still use Escanor's Skill 1 on Meliodas.

● Since Meliodas does not counter when pure attacking skills are used in Phase 1, it is NOT necessary to freeze him before attacking him every single time.

● When Phase 1 Elizabeth gets off her ultimate, the initial heal isn't going to heal much. The annoying part is the Cure that comes with the ult, which is a Heal-over-Time. When Cure is present on a decently low health Meliodas and Elizabeth, you might want to consider investing a rank 1 freeze to take it away.

● To complete the bonus mission of removing buffs, save rank 2/3 Cruel Sun to do that, instead of wasting a Merlin freeze.

● Ideally you would want to save at least 1 ult of main DPS or Merlin and 1 gold freeze going into Phase 2, so you can immediately nuke down Elizabeth in Phase 2 turn 1.

● If you want to achieve high score, it is pretty crucial to stack up Gowther's passive for the extra damage. You should stack it up after you killed Meliodas in Phase 1. Also, DO NOT stall the battle unnecessarily just to stack up the passive. The more turns you take, the less points you'll get.

● The textbook play for Phase 2 is to gold freeze and immediately take out either Elizabeth or Meliodas with an ult nuke, leaving the nuking of the other boss to the next turn. This makes Phase 2 a 2-turn play.
