Key Units Analysis


Unit Remark

Gowther		 Great unit to have on almost any raid. Gowther can rank up all allies' skills on Rank 2. On Rank 3, he buffs ATK, DEF and HP.

Meliodas		 One of the best DPS for this raid.
The ignite on S1 raises the overall DPS of both teams.
The infect on a Rank 2 S2 nullifies all forms of healing (regeneration, recovery and heal) on Mr Crab.
Using his ultimate skill when Mr Crab is frozen by a Rank 3 Merlin S2 (target will take 200% additional damage) allows him to deal massive damage to the weakness of Mr Crab, and often one-shotting a phase.

Vivian		 One of the best DPS for this raid, especially because it is relatively easy to max out her ultimate skill to do huge damage.
Similar to Meliodas, she can ignite the enemy on her S1.
Vivian's ultimate skill ignores the resistance of the target. You can use a rank 3 Merlin S2 (target will take 200% additional damage) to freeze Mr Crab at the start of phase 3 and follow up with Vivian's nuke first. Even if Vivian does not kill, she will be able to bring Mr Crab below 60% HP to remove the resistance buff for other damaging skills to hit harder.

Arthur		 Really good support unit for the raid.
Arthur's S1 seals healing skills on Rank 2 and Rank 3, dealing 150% more damage to Mr Crab.
His Rank 3 S2 also grants all allies with 30% HP, ATK and DEF buff as well as the immunity to counter the various debuffs Mr Crab puts out.

Merlin		 Merlin is both a good support and offense for Mr Crab.
She has as a boost of 30% increase in damage against Mr Crab, and a further 150% increase in damage on her S1 on Rank 2 and Rank 3.
Her S2 allows allies to nuke down Mr Crab at Rank 3 from full health.

Elizabeth & Hawk		 Elihawk is also a top tier DPS for this raid.
She seals buffs and debuffs on Rank 2 of S2 and removes Mr Crab's buff on Rank 3. She does good damage on her S1 at Rank 3 as well as the ulimate when maxed.

Sub Units Analysis


Unit Remark

Jericho		 Jericho's passive increases the ATK-related stats of all Strength Attribute allies by 10%.

Twigo		 Twigo's passive increases the HP of all Strength Attribute allies by 30%.

Dreyfus		 Dreyfus' passive increases the DEF of Strength Attribute allies by 60%.

Dogedo		 Dogedo's passive increases the DEF-related stats of Strength Attribute allies by 30%.

Filler Units Analysis


Unit Remark

Helbram		 Helbram can deal a decent amount of damage with his S1 on Rank 3. His S2 on Rank 2 and Rank 3, as well as his ultimate can also remove the resistance buff.

Elizabeth		 Elizabeth is able to seal attack skills on Rank 2 and Rank 3. She is also a decent healer as she has heals on both her S2, ult and passive. The ultimate gauge boost on her Rank 2 and Rank 3 S2 can also come in handy.

Ban		 Ban deals a good amount of damage especially on his S1, since it deals weakness damage to Mr Crab when there is a debuff (ignite, freeze, attack seal).
His ultimate skill also steals 50% of Mr Crab's ATK and DEF.

Benimaru		 Benimaru's S1 is the same as Ban.
He lowers the DEF of Mr Crab on his S2, so he can set himself up for his damage.
His passive also increases his damage for each skills he used.

Diane		 Diane is a great for both offense and support too.
Her S1 ignores Mr Crab's resistance, so even if Mr Crab has the resistance buffs/hardening stacked up, she will still be able to damage him normally.
Diane's S2 lowers Mr Crab's DEF-related stats, allowing more damage output from allies.
Her ultimate skill augments her pierce rate by 3x, making it easier to hit through the resistance/hardening stacks too.

Tips


1. Mr Crab takes 150% more damage from skills that seal healing skills (Merlin S1, Arthur S1)

2. There is a 100% increased in resistance buff when Mr Crab has above 60% HP. This makes it a lot harder to do damage to him when he's above 60%, so do ration out your attack cards.

3. Do not freeze/petrify/stun Mr Crab at the end of the turn - Mr Crab cleanses inability effects at the start of his turn.

4. Rank 1 and Rank 2 freezes are most optimally used to remove the resistance buffs; Rank 3 freezes can be used in conjunction with a huge nuke to finish the phase.

5. (For newer players - applicable in ALL raids): In most circumstances, do not use Gowther's rank up on yourself. Wait for either you or your partner to have a Rank 2 rank up, and follow it up with your own rank up to create a full hand of Rank 3 cards.
Possible scenario to rank up yourself: one of the teams is dying and you have a rank 2 buff (Arthur/Gilthunder/Helbram/Elaine), turning your Rank 2 buff into a Rank 3 buff would be a good choice in this situation.
Therefore, the general concensus is to "share the rank up and share the love", with a Rank 3 buff, everyone on the team benefits.
