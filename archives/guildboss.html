Guild Boss Season 2 Guide


Table of Content


NOTE: This guide was written and updated based on the JP version, therefore some units like Red Escanor will not be available in the Global version of the game.

In this guide, I will cover:
1. Basic information regarding Guild Boss Kelak (hereafter referred to as Kelak)
2. The scoring system and weekly updated missions
3. Recommended units
4. Kelak's skillset for Extreme difficulty

Information


1. The overall score system Guild Boss Kelak is based on highest point obtained and not cumulative points as seen from Guild Boss Season 1.

2. The highest point is an addition of base points, mission points and score points obtained.

  • Base points are given according to the difficulty - (Normal/Hard/Extreme) cleared
  • Mission points are given according to the number of bonus missions you cleared. There will be new bonus missions every week for all the difficulties.
  • Score points are calculated from scoring criteria

3. There are NO MORE unit restrictions - you can use the same units over and over again.

4. You will get personal rewards based on your highest point.

5. Kelak's attribute is strength (red).

6. The scoring criteria changes every week.

Kelak Extreme Information


Phase 1:
Attack: 4,817
Defense: 11,548
HP: 205,150

Certain buffs are applied in accordance with HP

Skills:
• Painful Chop: Inflict damage equal to 220%/360% of Kelak's Attack on one enemy, Infects for 1/2 turn(s).
• Heavy Stick: Inflicts damage equal to 210/350% of Kelak's Attack on one enemy, Poisons for 3 turns.
• Poison Gush: Inflicts damage equal to 180% of Kelak's Attack on all enemies and Lifesteals 100% of the damage.
• Dirty Shout: Decreases Defense-related stats of all enemies by 20% for 2 turns.
• Ultimate - Destroy Hope: Inflicts Weak Point damage equal to 385% of Kelak's Attack on one enemy.

Special Abilities:
• Purgatory Energy: Use "Sharp Knife" every 3 turns
• Ominous Aura: Use "Shake Poison" every 4 turns
• Skill Use Increase: x2.
• Cancel Incapacitation: At the start of Kelak's turn, cleanse the effects of freeze.
• Effect Immunity: Buff Disabling/Debuff Disabling, Recovery Skill Disabling/Infect/Petrify/Pressure/HP-related stats reduction effects.
