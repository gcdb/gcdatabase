Hawk's New Year Luck Card Event

■ Event Duration
30 December 2020 (Wednesday) after maintenance ~ 14 January 2021 (Thursday) before maintenance

■ Event Details
Clearing designated stages in game will drop [Hawk's Card Bag]. 1x [Hawk's Card Bag] will be consumed to open a card for reward.

■ Obtainable Location (max 30/day):
・ Main stage
・ SP Dungeon
・ Training Grotto
・ Death Match

・ Knighthood Boss Battle

■ Obtainable Location (max 40):
・ Special Boss Battle Return Exchange Shop

■ Obtainable Location (max 1/day):
・ Silver Coin Shop (3 [Hawk's Card Bag]/1 silver coin)

■ Event Details
Every stage will have a special reward that you will choose 1 out of the 2 before beginning the stage. On the 2nd, 5th and 8th stage, you will get double the reward if you open the special reward card within 5 tries.
