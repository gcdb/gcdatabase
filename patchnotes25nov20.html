You can open max 9 boxes a day. From the 9 boxes, 1 of the following boxes will appear depending on probability: [Nothing Box], [Normal Box] and [Special Box]. ■ System Changes ■

1. Battle Menu Changes

2. Fort Solgres System Changes

2.1 Event Dungeon is now unified under one dungeon, you can obtain pendants and EXP potion from this dungeon.

2.2 New dungeon [Time Limit Dungeon] added. Opening the dungeon will allow you to play in it for 1h without limit. You can obtain anvils in this dungeon.

2.3 [Power Up], [Evolution], [Gold Dungeon] content changes

There will be 1~5 stages. Difficulties are adjusted as well as AP consumption.

2.4 [Gold Dungeon] system changes

Removing the [Gold Dungeon] keys, AP will be used to enter the dungeon. Existing keys will be converted to attribute dungeon keys at 2:1 ratio.

3. [Knighthood Boss Battle] System Changes

3.1 You don't have to be a part of Knighthood to take part in the battle now.

3.2 You will need to spend AP to play the battle and awakened equipment can be obtained

3.3 Hero EXP, rank EXP and affection can be obtained via the battle now.
