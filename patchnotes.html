8. Tower of Challenge

※ Accessible after clearing Chapter 10 episode 155

■ Event Duration
11 June 2020 (Thursday) after maintenance ~ 15 July 2020 (Wednesday) 23:59

■ What is the Tower of Challenge?
[Tower of Challenge] consists of 25 floors, and you will be able to obtain rewards such as [Super Awakening Coins], gold, diamonds, Part 1 and Part 2 SSR tickets. The rewards are only available upon first clear, if you change the challenge party halfway through, you will not be able to obtain the rewards you have already gotten

■ Note
※ You can only use 4 characters to form the challenge party, association characters can be changed
※ At special floors (save points), you will be able to edit your previous party of 4
If you change the character before clearing a special floor (save point), you will be returned to the last save point
※ Special floor (save points) are at every 5 floors, and will be automatically saved, if there is no save points, you will return to the first floor
※ The [Tower of Challenge] is seasonal, and when the season ends the rewards and cleared floors will be reset

■ System Changes and Addition ■

1. New hero with engraving:
Drole

2. Gear ★5 Awakening Bonus

Upon ★5 awakening SSR gear, you will receive 1 diamond.
Gear awakened prior to the update (including UR gear) will be counted and the respective diamond rewards will be given (collect the reward between 11 June ~ 10 July) in the mail.
The awakening bonus can be obtained infinitely.

3. Addition of Super Awakening System

You are now able to super awaken units that are already full awakened (★6). You will be able to further super awaken the unit based on the ultimate level. (★6 awakened gives 1 super awakening chance + higher ultimate level will give super awakening chances too)

Materials needed for super awakening: Super Awakening Coin (obtained from Tower of Challenge), Platinum Coin, Gold Coin, Silver Coin.
※ The gauge increase will differ based on the rarity of the coins used
※ All coins can be used except for Fes Coins
※ The coins required will differ based on the character rairty
※ Using the same coin as the unit you are super awakening will not increase the gauge more
※ Obtainable ocation for Super Awakening Coin can be changed in the future
※ Extra gauge will not be carried over to the next level
