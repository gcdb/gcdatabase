■ [Physical Clone] Lostvayne Meliodas Skills Info (rank 3)

Skill 1 - Division Impact: Inflicts damage equal to 500% of Attack on one enemy. Fills Ultimate Move Gauge by 2 orb(s).

■ <The Ten Commandments> Fraudrin of Selflessness Skills Info (rank 3)

Skill 1 - White Eyes: Inflicts damage equal to 250% of Attack on one enemy. Extort 50% Attack and Defense for 2 turn(s).

Skill 2 - Slash: Cancels Buffs and Stances on all enemies and inflicts damage equal to 210% of Attack.

Ult - Dark Explosion: Inflicts damage equal to 630% of Attack on one enemy and depletes 3 Ultimate Move Gauge orb(s).

Passive/Unique - Contempt: In PVP, heroes that have lower CC than Fraudrin will not gain Ultimate Move Gauge for 2 turns (Applies when entering battle)

Obtainable Location
