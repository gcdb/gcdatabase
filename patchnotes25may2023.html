25 May 2023 Patch Notes (THE IMPORTANT STUFF ONLY)

■ LR Evolution

Condition for LR evolution: Lv.100 and SA 6

LR units will have their passive, skills and ult effec changed and stat increased

Seal of Legend material can be farmed in Labyrinth.

■ New PvE content - Hero Arena

Battle in one of the 5 leagues against 3 enemies. Each enemy has 5 teams of 4 members each on the defense. When all defense teams are defeated you win.

No penalties for losing the battle, you can rematch. If the difficulty is too hard you can reselect the league. The event runs for 4 weeks and challenges resets weekly. Every season there will be units getting buffs for this event.
