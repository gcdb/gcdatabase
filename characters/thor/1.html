Passive/Unique

Hammer of Judgement | 裁きのハンマー
At the start of the battle, applies Divine Lightning on the hero and decreases Applicable Allies' damage taken by 30% for 2 turns. When Divine Lightning reaches its maximum value, the effect is removed and the hero gains Thundergod's Judgment for 3 turns.
In addition, for each Applicable Ally participating in battle, all of the hero's stats are increased by 5%. For each Buff or Debuff applied to Applicable Allies, the hero's damage dealt increases by 4%, up to 40%.
