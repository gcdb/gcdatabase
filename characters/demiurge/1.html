Passive/Unique

Devil's Chancellor | 悪魔の諸相
At the start of the battle, Mind Control is applied on the enemy with the highest Attack and an enemy that has a lower Attack than the hero. The hero is given the additional race Demon. For each Demon ally participating in battle, their basic stats increase by 5% and their damage taken decreases by 10%.
At the start of the allies' turn, Blazing Inferno is applied on all enemies for 2 turns and the hero's damage dealt increases by 50% when attacking with an attribute advantage. When an enemy uses a Debuff Atk Skill, their Max HP decreases by 30% for 1 turn.
