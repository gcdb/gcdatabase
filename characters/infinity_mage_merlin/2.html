Passive/Unique

Mage of the Sea | 海の魔術士
Applies 1 random Elemental Effect on an enemy unaffected by Elemental Effects at the start of the allies' turn, then increases the hero's basic stats by 7% for each Elemental Effect on the enemy. When an ally uses a skill on an enemy affected by an Elemental Effect, it applies the same additional Elemental Effect for 3 turns. At 4 Elemental Effect stacks, it removes Elemental Effects, then applies the corresponding Debuff effect of the Element for 1 turn.
In addition, basic stats of Unknown race allies are increased by 5% for each one participating in battle. If an Elemental Effect is removed due to a Unique ability or Holy Relic effect, it inflicts damage equal to 15% of the enemy's remaining HP for each Elemental Effect removed. (Does not apply to instances where the target survives due to Unique ability or Holy Relic effect)
(Rockslide: Stun / Combustion: 50% of Max HP as Additional Damage / Diffusion: Attack Disable / Gust: 100% Crit Chance Decrease)
