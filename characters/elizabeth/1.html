Recommended Usage
Elizabeth can heal passively from the sub slot. She can be used on the sub slot for early game Crimson raids to provide extra sustains, or to be used on the sub slot for soloing Crimson. She also has attack card sealing skill and gives ultimate gauge. Overall, although she can be put on main slot and work as a healer, she is mainly seen on the sub slot.
Recommended Gears
For main slot: ATK/DEF
Substats: ATK, DEF, HP
The amount she heals is based off her ATK stats, so it's better to have her on ATK/DEF for more healing.
For sub slot: HP/DEF
Substats: ATK, DEF, HP
For extra Combat Class/CP, give her HP/DEF if she's in the sub slot
