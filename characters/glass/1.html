Warrior of the Vassal Fan | 扇の眷属器の勇者
Increases the hero's basic stats by 7% for each Unknown race ally participating in battle. The hero gains 1 Soul Power whenever an Unknown race ally uses a skill. Conversely, 1 Soul Power is removed whenever the hero uses a skill. Whenever 1 Soul Power is removed, the hero's Pierce Rate increases by 30%, up to 90%. When the Soul Power stack is maxed out, the effects are removed, the hero's Crit Chance increases by 30%, and their Pierce Rate increases by 150% for 3 turns.
