Passive/Unique

Bloodthrist | 血の飢え
Increases the hero's basic stats by 7% for every Unknown ally in battle. When the hero deals damage, or if an ally takes damage from a skill during the enemy's turn, the hero's HP related stats increase by 7%, up to 3 times. If there are 3 of this effect at the start of the allies' turn, they are removed and the hero gains the Berserk effect for 1 turn. In addition, targets Bleed for 2 turns when the hero inflicts damage using an all-target Attack Skill.
