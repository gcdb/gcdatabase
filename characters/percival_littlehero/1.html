Passive/Unique

Orchestrator of Life | 新たな始まり
At the start of the battle, Recovery Factor is applied on Applicable Allies for 1 turn and all of the hero's stats increase by 5% for each Applicable Ally participating in battle. When the hero uses skills 2 times during the allies' turn, Applicable Allies gain Recovery Factor for 1 turn and have 2 of their Debuffs removed. In addition, the hero gains 1 Unity's Strength effect for each surviving Applicable Ally at the start of the allies' turn. Each Unity's Strength effect increases allies' Defense-related stats by 8%. When 4 or more Unity's Strength effects are removed the hero gains Firm Volition for 2 turns. (Limit once)
