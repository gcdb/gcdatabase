Recommended Usage
Hugo has both weakness and burst on his skill and ultimate skill. However, since he is a R unit, it does limit his usability. His passive requires him to be on the field to be activated, so he's not an optimal sub unit too..
Recommended Gears
For main slot: ATK/DEF
Substats: ATK, DEF, HP
In the very few scenarios you could use Hugo, you would want ATK/DEF to maxamize his weakness or burst damage.
