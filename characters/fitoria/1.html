Passive/Unique

Ruler of the Filolials | フィロリアルの支配者
Increases Crit Defense of Unknown race allies by 60% for 1 turn at the start of the battle. At the start of the allies' turn, applies The Queen's Authority on Unknown race allies for 2 turns (limit once) and increases all stats of the hero by 5% for each Unknown race ally participating in battle.
In addition, the hero's damage dealt increases by 50% when using Gale. When an Unknown race ally uses a skill to deal damage to the enemy during the allies' turn, the hero gains Sharp Talons effects equal to the number of enemies who took Crit Damage. If the hero has maxed out the stack of Sharp Talons at the start of the allies' turn, they are removed and the hero gains The Queen's Strike for 2 turns.
