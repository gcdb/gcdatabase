Recommended Usage
Gowther is decently useful for early game players as his kit centers around lowering the damage of enemy by ranking down or reducing the ultimate damage. Besides that, he is mostly used as a Associated with Escanor, especially when red Gwother and Merlin are unable as Association if they are put on the main team with Escanor.
