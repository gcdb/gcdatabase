Recommended Usage
Vivian is a nuker with Ultimate Move disabling utility, with her Unique/Passive boosts her damage by a fair amount too. She can be used as the damage dealer in Crimson Death Match as a F2P friendly option before getting more meta units such as red Derieri and red Demon Meliodas.
