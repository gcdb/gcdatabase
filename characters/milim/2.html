Passive/Unique

Supreme Power | 次元の違う力
At the start of the battle, applies 2 stacks of Dragon Power on the hero, then Dragon Protection for 3 turns. Increases allies' Defense-Related Stats by 20% for each stack of Dragon Power on self. 1 instance of Dragon Power is removed each time an ally uses a skill or this hero takes damage from an enemy's skill use on the enemy's turn.
Additionally, all stats of the hero increase by 7% each time a stack of Dragon Power is lost, up to a maximum of 28%. Once all the instances are removed, Dragon Rage is applied to the hero for 3 turns. When Dragon Rage expires, 2 stacks of Dragon Power are applied to the hero once again.
