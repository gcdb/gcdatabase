Passive/Unique

Guardians of the Earth and Forest | 大地と森の守護者
Increases basic stats of Applicable Allies participating in battle by 15% and increases all stats of the hero duo by 7% for each Applicable Ally.
At the start of the battle, applies Wrath of the Earth on all enemies, then applies Giant Queen's Protection on the hero duo for 1 turn.
Whenever an enemy reaches the maximum Wrath of the Earth value, the hero duo gains 1 Wrath of the Fairy King effect, up to a maximum of 3.
When the hero duo uses an all-target attack other than the Ultimate Move on an enemy with the Wrath of the Earth effect, the enemy takes additional damage equal to 20% of the accumulated value.
1 Wrath of the Fairy King effect is removed from the hero duo when an Applicable Ally takes damage from an enemy's skill during the enemy's turn.
If there is a Petrified enemy, Applicable Allies' final damage taken from enemy skills decreases by 20%.
