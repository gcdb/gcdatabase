Passive/Unique

Journey of Enlightenment | 自分探しの旅
Increases basic stats of allies with the [The Seven Deadly Sins] characteristic by 5% for each one participating in battle. Applies Rank Enhancement on the hero for 2 turns each time the hero uses a Buff Skill. Increase allies' HP by 2%, up to 20% for each Debuff removed.
In addition, if 3 alies of different races each use a skill once or more during the allies' turn, it increases the hero's skill ranks.
(Heroes with an additional race count as 2 races.)
