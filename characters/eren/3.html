Passive/Unique

Desire for Freedom | 自由への渇望
For every Giant or Human ally participating in battle, increases the hero's basic stats by 10%. The hero is given the additional race Giant, is affected by Giants' Unique Abilities, and heals Giant allies that have taken damage during the enemies' turn for 10% of their Max HP at the start of the allies' turn. When the hero attacks and enemy, every Buff applied to the enemy increases the hero's damage dealt by 30%. When an ally with an attribute advtange uses a skill to attack the enemy, that ally's Attack-related stats are increased by 20%.
