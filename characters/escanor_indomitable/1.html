Passive/Unique

The Only Sun | ひとつの太陽
Applies Burning Sun on the hero at the start of the battle. For 1 turn, the hero does not take more than 50% of their Max HP as damage from skills during the enemy's turn. If the hero dies, allies gain Burning Sun for 3 turns.
In addition, all of the hero's stats are increased by 5% for each applicable ally participating in battle, and the hero's Attack-Related Stats are increased by 20% for each applicable ally that dies. The hero is always immune to stat decrease effects.
