6. The Four Archangel Amikuji (Ghost Leg) Event

■ Event Duration
29 October 2020 (Thursday) after maintenance ~ 12 November 2020 (Thursday) before maintenance

■ Event Details
Obtain ticket to play the event by completing: free stages, main stages, boss battles, SP dungeon, Training Grotto, Death Matches, Seven Catastrophes boss battle/death match, Tower of Challenge. You can get up to 30 tickets/day. You can also use 1 silver coin to exchange for 2 tickets for up to 5 exchanges/day.
You will need 10 tickets to play 1 round of the event for a max of 2 times/day. Various rewards such as anvils, awakening stone, grimoires, skip tickets, treasure chests, character growth materials can be obtained.
