■ System Changes ■

1. Elite PVP Rules

2. You are now able to invite players to Knighthoods if the player is not in a Knighthood. You can turn ON/OFF Knighthood invitation notification in the setting.

3. Brawl AI settings added. You can customize according to survivability, decapacitation, skill movement, ultimate move etc.

4. Equipment enhancement system changes:
4.1 Equipment upgrade will no longer fail.
4.2 When using continuous upgrade, you can choose the stage of awakening and upgrade level now.

5. Knighthood Boss Season 1~3 are now added as playable stages. You can earn various rewards from clearing these.
